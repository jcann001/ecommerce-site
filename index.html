Succulents

Succulents have the ability to thrive on limited water sources, such as mist and dew, which makes them equipped to survive in an ecosystem that contains scarce water sources.

Perennials

Plants that persist for many growing seasons. Generally the top portion of the plant dies back each winter and regrows the following spring.

Ferns

Flowerless plants which has feathery or leafy fronds and reproduces by spores released from the undersides of the fronds. Ferns have a vascular system for the transport of water and nutrients.

Exotics

Plants that has been introduced to an area from outside its native range. Several different variants of species are included from venus fly traps to bamboo.

Cactus

Cactus will steal your heart with their charming, prickly forms and showy flowers. They require some caution when handling, but are super low maintenance.
